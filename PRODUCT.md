# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors are classmates, teachers, collaborators, family, and people who want a quick read on Herschel as a student developer. They arrive with low patience and should understand his identity, links, and current direction without digging.

## Product Purpose

This is Herschel's personal GitHub Pages site: a clean public home for his developer profile, early projects, and learning trajectory. Success means the page feels credible, calm, and easy to update as real projects and achievements are added.

## Brand Personality

Clean, quiet, modern. The site should feel composed and spacious rather than loud, flashy, or overly corporate.

## Anti-references

Avoid neon gamer styling, generic resume templates, dense corporate portfolio grids, fake achievement language, and overdecorated AI-looking landing page patterns.

## Design Principles

- Let negative space carry confidence.
- Say only what can be supported by the current profile.
- Make the GitHub profile and future project path obvious.
- Favor quiet hierarchy over decorative effects.
- Keep the structure easy to maintain as Herschel adds real work.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, clear focus states, responsive layouts down to narrow mobile screens, and reduced-motion support for any transitions.
